About X1coin

X1coin (X1C) is an alternative cryptocurrency project running on the SHA-256 proof-of-work algorithm, designed for payments, receipts, and value storage. The project aims to extend the potential of SHA-256 beyond traditional uses and foster a connected community driving real-world adoption and utility. 0

The project was launched in early 2024 and emphasizes collective progress, belief in shared goals, and tangible results through united actions. 1
